About Me

Hello! My name is Grace Caroline Reiland (she / her), and I am from the Pittsburgh area. I live about twenty minutes south of Pitt, and I chose to go to school here because I have always loved Pittsburgh. I am very passionate about my relationships to those close to me, and it is very important to stay connected with them. Beyond my parents, my immediate family consists of my sister, Sarah; my dog, Zoey; and my cat, Milo. I additionally consider some of my closest friends to be my family.

Although my desired field in terms of occupation is a career in Psychology, I believe this can be generalized to a greater topic of people overall. I am mainly concerned with how people perceive, behave, and relate to one another. Studying psychology gives me a means to do so, and it provides me with the potential to create change. In my free time, I mainly enjoy being with others, playing guitar, listening to music, going outside, travelling, thrifting, and cafe hopping.
